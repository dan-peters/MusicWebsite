events


Dan has more than 10 years of experience performing at private events including weddings, corporate functions, and private parties.

As a classically trained pianist with a jazz degree, he can perform a variety of musical styles suitable to your event including:

  • Wedding ceremony
    • Jazz standards (like Misty, The Nearness of You, Fly Me to the Moon)
    • Instrumental piano renditions of popular songs (like Beatles, Elton John, Stevie Wonder)
  • Cocktail hour
    • Solo jazz piano
    • Jazz duo or trio
  • Events/Parties
    • Jazz (solo or with duo/trio)
    • Folk/singer-songwriter (original music and covers)

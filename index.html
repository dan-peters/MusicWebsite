Bio

Dan Peters is a pianist and singer-songwriter, born and raised in coastal Massachusetts, who joined the Austin music scene in 2020. He leads a double life as an environmental scientist and a musician, playing piano with several Austin groups including a jazz trio and Nossas Novas, a Brazilian jazz and rock band. As a songwriter, Dan gravitates towards his folk and rock influences (Big Thief, Wilco, and John Prine to name a few), opting for simpler, memorable melodies with thoughtful lyrics that convey his love for nature and a knack for finding meaning in everyday observations.

ABOUT

A jazz pianist and singer-songwriter new on the Austin scene, Dan Peters draws on a wide range of musical influences to craft his own authentic sound. Seamlessly folding in jazz harmonies with equal parts folk, rock, and Americana, Dan’s warm compositions and lyrics are full of curiosity about nature and the human experience.

Dan’s musical influences include:
Leif Vollebeck
Brad Mehldau / Chris Thile
Bill Evans
Charles Mingus
Mandolin Orange
Paul Simon
Wilco / Jeff Tweedy
Radiohead

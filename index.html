Dan Peters

Bio

Recordings

A pianist & singer-songwriter new on the scene in Austin, TX, Dan Peters folds in jazz harmonies with equal parts folk, rock, and Americana to craft his own authentic sound. Dan’s warm compositions and lyrics are full of curiosity about nature and the human experience. Listeners have described his music as “someone walking in the rain through an old timey downtown.”

Selected venues

Mozart’s Coffee Roasters (Austin, TX) Room 43 (Chicago, IL) Carnegie Hall (New York, NY) Salem Jazz and Soul Festival (Salem, MA) Marblehead Festival of Arts (Marblehead, MA) Chianti Jazz Club (Beverly, MA) Shaolin Liu Performance Center (Rockport, MA)
